Rhinestone Lifestyle was born out of a desire to live the life I dreamed of while keeping my values and budget balanced. Diamond dreams on a rhinestone budget.

I create one of a kind and limited edition products that balance being eco-friendly and wallet friendly. They are built to last for years instead of just a season. As an example, I try to source fabrics from places where they might otherwise end up in a landfill, such as mill outlets, remnants, and other fabric reuse. I am working on turning the fabric scraps from my projects into filling for dog beds which will then be donated to shelters.

I hope you'll find some items that make your life shine!

Thank you!
--Joanie, owner and creator at Rhinestone Lifestyle
